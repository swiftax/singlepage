What inspired you to become a web developer?

Although my career began as an accountant, my first small business job was as a CFO for a graphic design firm called PerfectHQ (Formerly Known As Enhanced Labs). They have done high-end design work for Microsoft and ADP to name a few. For a few years, I had been brainstorming ideas for web applications, but didn't know any programming languages. I decided just go for it and begin teaching myself and going through coding schools.

What coding languages are being talked about most right now?

AngularJS and node.js are the most talked about right now and specifically the mean stack due to performance for the latest web applications.

What is your main goal with web development?

To bring ideas to life for entrepreneurs and to provide them with a good foundation with their web application and business structure.

Where were you born and raised?

I lived in Washington State until the age of 19 and then I moved to South Florida. Big change! Summer is year-round.
